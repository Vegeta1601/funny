🧭 Extremely Official Travel Tips

01

Embrace getting lost. Every wrong turn in a foreign city is secretly a shortcut to the best story you'll tell for the next 10 years. GPS is for quitters. (Just kidding. Use GPS. But also get lost.)

02

Say yes to the weird food. That mystery street food? That fermented thing the locals love? EAT IT. Your stomach will forgive you eventually. Probably.

03

Talk to strangers. (Yes, we know what we were told as kids.) Fellow travelers and locals have the best tips, the wildest stories, and sometimes free snacks. Highly recommend.

04

Miss your flight? New adventure unlocked. Every travel disaster becomes a trophy. "The time I slept in a Thai airport" beats any Netflix documentary.

05

You don't need to "be ready." No one who ever had the trip of their life was 100% ready. Pack a bag, book something, figure out the rest on the way. Future You will send back thank-you notes.
